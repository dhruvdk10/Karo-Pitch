About Karo Pitch

Karo Pitch is a startup discovery and investment platform designed to bridge the gap between promising founders and potential investors. We help early-stage startups present their ideas, gain visibility, and access mentorship, funding, and strategic partnerships. Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs across India especially from emerging cities to turn ideas into successful businesses.
